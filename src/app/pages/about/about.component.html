About

Meet our founders, Ruby and Millie. Ruby and Millie have one goal to deliver technology solutions that help people get their work done so they have more time to play with their dogs, and well if they have to, their cats.

The application nodebucket, a task management solution, came to them one day when it was apparent that someone had forgotten to give them there daily walk.

With nodebucket you can track what you have to-do, have started and what you have completed. The simple drag and drop interface allows you to quickly update the status of the task.
