About

Meet our founders, Ruby and Millie. Ruby and Millie have a goal, to deliver technology solutions that help people get their work done, so they have more time to play with their dogs, and well, if they have to, their cats.

The nodebucket application, a task management solution, came to them one day when it was apparent that someone had forgotten to take them on their daily walk.

If only there were a way for their people to easily see what they have to do today.

Use nodebucket to track what you have to-do, have started, and what you have completed. Create a task, it will automatically be added to your To-do list. The simple drag and drop interface allows you to quickly order and update the status of the task by putting it in the proper column To-do, Doing, or Done. The application is secured by your unique id, making sure that only you can see and manage your tasks. nodebucket is designed to run on your desktop, tablet or phone so you will always be able to view and manage your tasks.

nodebucket was written and is maintained by bLabWare. It was built using the MEAN stack, Mongo DB, Express, Angular, and Nodejs. For more information about bLabWare check out our sites on the various social media platforms or email us using the links below.
